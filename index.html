The Problem with Legal Documents Today

Legal documents are the backbone of justice, but their length, complexity, and language make them inaccessible to many people, creating significant barriers to justice and equity.

Complex Legal Language

Dense terminology and legal jargon that is inaccessible to non-lawyers, creating confusion and misinterpretation.

85% struggle to understand legal documents

Time-Consuming Reviews

Lawyers spend excessive time drafting and reviewing documents, reducing efficiency and increasing costs.

40–60% of lawyer time spent on review

Limited Accessibility

Millions worldwide lack minimum literacy or face language barriers that prevent access to legal information.

775M people lack minimum literacy

About NyayaSulabh

NyayaSulabh is a purpose-driven legal-tech platform committed to democratizing access to justice. We leverage cutting-edge AI and natural language processing to transform complex legal documents into clear, comprehensive, multilingual summaries that anyone can understand.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex legal jargon and public understanding, ensuring justice is accessible, equitable, and transparent for everyone, regardless of background or literacy level.

We believe in AI for public good, empowering communities through knowledge and making legal understanding accessible to all.
